What Are Fire Watch Security Services?

Fire watch services involve trained security guards who monitor your property when fire alarms, sprinklers, or suppression systems are not working properly. These guards patrol the area, look for fire hazards, and are ready to alert authorities if needed. Why Fire Watch Is Important for Florida Businesses

  • Legal compliance: Florida law requires fire watch coverage when systems fail.
  • Safety assurance: Guards reduce risks by spotting hazards early.
